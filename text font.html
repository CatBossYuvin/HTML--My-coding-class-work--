Cats


Cats, scientifically known as Felis catus, are small, domesticated carnivorous mammals, often kept as pets. They are characterized by their slender, muscular bodies, sharp claws, keen senses of hearing and smell, and excellent night vision. Their coats come in various colors and patterns, including tabby, tortoiseshell, and calico

dogs

Dogs, often called "man's best friend," are domesticated mammals known for their loyalty and companionship. They are characterized by their furry coats, four legs, and a tail, and they communicate through barking. Dogs display a wide range of physical characteristics, with over 400 recognized breeds exhibiting diverse sizes, shapes, and colors. Beyond their physical attributes, dogs possess keen senses of smell, hearing, and sight, making them adept at hunting, guarding, and various other tasks
