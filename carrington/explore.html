The Destination: Northland

Connect with Cape

Carrington connects you with cape, caves, coast, culture, and New Zealand charm. Northland boasts some of the most authentic, spectacular New Zealand adventures and experiences, with a thousand journeys starting at Carrington.

Discover Caves

Connect with caves by entering Riko Riko, the world’s largest sea cave. Experience underwater wonderlands with subtropical diving through one of the many wreck dives around Northland.
